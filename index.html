Paul J. Raymond

Paul earned his Juris Doctor from the University of Denver Sturm College of Law and his B.A. in Recreation and Outdoor Education, with a minor in History, from Western Colorado University, graduating magna cum laude.

His legal work has focused on water rights, transactions, adjudications, land use planning, real estate matters, and development issues for municipalities, special districts, ranch and large property owners, and developers.

That mix of legal training and outdoor experience also makes him a natural fit for recreation businesses, rental operations, and outfitters looking for practical counsel on risk, access, and operations.
