Meet the Attorney Behind the Practice

Paul J. Raymond

Paul founded The Law Office of Paul J. Raymond in 2026 to help serve a growing gap in legal expertise in rural central Colorado.

His roots run deep in the Buena Vista and Gunnison outdoor communities. An avid mountain biker and fly fisherman, he embraces Colorado's wild beauty while focusing on water and property rights.

Paul's goal is to provide flexible and low-cost legal services to areas of Colorado without access to legal resources.
